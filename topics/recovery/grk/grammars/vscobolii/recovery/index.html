GRK recovery scripts for VS Cobol II

The recovery scripts are meant to derive a grammar that is useful for regeneration of an improved IBM VS Cobol II application programming reference.
  • root.fst Re-engineer the overall syntactical structure of Cobol programs, i.e., the division and section structure.

  • leafs.fst Re-engineer the leafs of the Cobol grammar, i.e., names and expressions.

  • ed.fst Re-engineer the environment division in a detailed manner.

  • fd-entries.fst Re-engineer the file-description entries.

  • dd-entries.fst Re-engineer the data-description entries.

  • stats.fst Re-engineer the statement syntax.
